About Me

Hello, my name is Orfeo Kaplow.
As a beginner game developer, I have always had a passion for creating interactive and fun experiences for others to enjoy, especially for my friends and me. At 16 years old, I have already shown a strong interest in computer science and am looking to further my education in this field. I am now 17 and almost done with school.

My curiosity and drive to learn new things have served me well so far, and I am confident that they will continue to do so as I continue on my journey as a game developer. Whether I am working on small personal project or doing something as a team on group projects, my dedication and hard work have always been a driving force behind my games.

With a bright future ahead of me, I am excited to see all that I will accomplish as a game developer and computer scientist. I am confident that my skills and determination will lead me to great success in the industry, and I can't wait to see what the future holds.
